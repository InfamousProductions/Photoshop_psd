
How to use:

 Open the PSD file

Change the size

 convert to smart object and resize to your format or
 Go to "Image - Image Size" put in your size and keep the "Scale Styles" checked

Change the text and the font

Change Text:
 Select the Type tool and click on the text and change the text to your own.
 Now make the upper layer invisible and click on the other layer to change it with the same text.

Change Font:
 Select both text layers and change the font and the size of the font.

Change the colors of the styles

 Select the first layer that comes after background.
 double click the layer for layer styles
 Click on the layer style "Stroke" and change the color of the gradient.
