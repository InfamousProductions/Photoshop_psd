In Photoshop a layer style refers to the two-dimensional and three-dimensional effects applied to each layer. A filter is an extension of these layer styles which changes the overall image on a layer by layer basis in a wide variety of ways. 

INSTALLATION 

    * Styles are in .asl format. After the download copy and install them in the C:\Program Files\Adobe\Adobe Photoshop CS3\Presets\Styles folder.

For more and detailed infos read here:

http://graphicssoft.about.com/od/photoshop/qt/installcontent.htm
